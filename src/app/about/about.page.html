Why create this app?

I'm an ex-cardiac nurse that stumbled my way through coding and ux design.

I’ve been constantly refining this small (but valuable) tool since working at Metro North Cardiac Rehabilitation in Brisbane, Australia.

I built this because I wish I had this at the time I was working in Cardiac Rehabilitation.
